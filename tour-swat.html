4 Days Trip to Swat Valley

Home / Tours / Swat Valley Tour

Duration

4 Days / 3 Nights

Price

Rs15,000

Accommodation

3-Star Hotels

Tour Details

Explore the lush green valleys, crystal clear rivers, and majestic mountains of Swat, often called the "Switzerland of Pakistan." This 4-day tour takes you through the most scenic spots in this beautiful region.

Tour Highlights

  • Visit the historic Swat Museum and archaeological sites
  • Explore the beautiful Malam Jabba Ski Resort
  • Experience the serenity of Kalam Valley and Mahodand Lake
  • Enjoy the crystal clear waters of Swat River
  • Discover local handicrafts and traditional Swati culture

Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Swat

Arrive in Mingora, Swat. Check-in at your hotel and enjoy a welcome dinner. Brief orientation about the tour.

Day 2

Mingora and Malam Jabba

Visit Swat Museum and archaeological sites in Mingora. After lunch, drive to Malam Jabba Ski Resort and enjoy the panoramic views of the valley.

Day 3

Kalam Valley

Full-day excursion to Kalam Valley. Visit Mahodand Lake (subject to weather conditions) and enjoy the beautiful scenery along Swat River.

Day 4

Departure

After breakfast, check-out from the hotel. Visit local handicraft shops for souvenir shopping before departure.
