Vehicle Details

Our Toyota Coaster Bus is the perfect solution for group travel across Pakistan. Spacious, comfortable, and reliable, this vehicle can accommodate up to 22 passengers, making it ideal for family reunions, corporate outings, or group tours to Pakistan's most beautiful destinations.

Vehicle Features

  • Spacious seating with ample legroom for long journeys
  • Powerful air conditioning system for comfort in all weather
  • Reclining seats with adjustable headrests
  • Entertainment system with LCD screen and audio
  • Large windows for panoramic views
  • Ample storage space for luggage

Rental Includes

  • Professional, experienced driver
  • Fuel for up to 150 km per day
  • Vehicle insurance
  • Complimentary bottled water
  • Regular maintenance and cleaning
  • 24/7 customer support

Not Included

  • Additional fuel beyond 150 km/day
  • Driver accommodation for overnight trips
  • Driver meals
  • Toll taxes and parking fees
  • Additional stops not in the agreed itinerary

Perfect For

  • Group tours to Northern Pakistan
  • Corporate team outings and retreats
  • School and university field trips
  • Wedding parties and family gatherings
  • Religious pilgrimages and group visits

Booking Information

For the Coaster Bus, we recommend booking at least 7 days in advance, especially during peak tourist season (May-September). A 50% advance payment is required to confirm your reservation, with the balance due before the journey begins.

For multi-day trips, please note that driver accommodation should be arranged by the client or can be added to your package for an additional fee.
