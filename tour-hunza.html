5 Days Trip to Hunza Valley

Home / Tours / Hunza Valley Explorer

Duration

5 Days / 4 Nights

Tour Type

Group Tour

Group Size

10-15 People

Price

PKR 45,000 / person

Tour Details

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Hunza Valley, known for its stunning landscapes, rich culture, and friendly locals. This 5-day tour takes you through the most picturesque locations in the region.

Highlights

  • Visit the historic Baltit Fort and Altit Fort
  • Explore the stunning Attabad Lake
  • Experience local Hunza culture and cuisine
  • Trek to Eagle's Nest for panoramic views
  • Visit Passu Cones and the Hussaini Suspension Bridge

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Hunza

Arrive in Hunza and check into your hotel. Enjoy a welcome dinner and briefing about the tour.

Day 2: Baltit & Altit Forts

Visit the historic Baltit Fort and Altit Fort. Explore the local market and enjoy traditional Hunza cuisine.

Day 3: Attabad Lake & Passu

Visit the stunning blue waters of Attabad Lake. Continue to Passu to see the famous Passu Cones.

Day 4: Eagle's Nest & Duikar

Early morning trek to Eagle's Nest for sunrise views. Visit Duikar village and interact with locals.

Day 5: Departure

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and depart with beautiful memories of Hunza Valley.

What's Included

  • 4 nights accommodation in 3-star hotels
  • Daily breakfast and dinner
  • Transportation in air-conditioned vehicle
  • Professional English-speaking guide
  • Entrance fees to all mentioned attractions
  • Welcome and farewell dinners

What's Not Included

  • Airfare to/from Gilgit
  • Lunches (unless specified)
  • Personal expenses
  • Travel insurance
  • Optional activities not mentioned in the itinerary
  • Tips and gratuities
